How to Play Battleship?

⚓ Type in a cell number (eg: A3, b5) in the Fire box below.

⚓ Click 'Fire' or press Enter to attack the entered cell.

About Ships

⚓ One Ship is made up of three cells.

⚓ You have to hit all three cells to sink a ship.

⚓ There are 3 ships on the board. Sink all three to win the game.
